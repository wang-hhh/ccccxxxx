Who Are We

We are a next-generation media company based in Irvine, California. Our mission is to tell powerful stories that resonate with global audiences — anytime, anywhere, on any screen.

We are preparing to launch a brand-new short drama streaming app — crafted for today’s mobile-first viewers who crave emotionally gripping, bite-sized content. Our platform will feature a rich library of addictive series that are easy to watch and hard to stop.

Looking ahead, we are also developing a digital reading platform: a mobile app and website for serialized novels across genres like romance, fantasy, and suspense — designed for the modern reader who loves immersive, fast-paced storytelling.

What We Do

Produce and distribute short-form drama series via our original streaming app.

Curate and publish high-quality serialized fiction through our upcoming reading platform.

Partner with creators to bring diverse voices to global audiences.
